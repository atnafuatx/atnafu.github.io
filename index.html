Mexico City, Mexico

My Name is Atnafu Lambebo Tonja I am PhD Student at Centro de Investigación en Computación (CIC), Instituto Politécnico Nacional, Mexico. I have M.Sc in Information Technology from Wolaita Sodo University, Ethiopia and I have B.Sc. in Computer Science from Madawalabu University, Ethiopia.

My primary research interest is Natural Language Processing(NLP), particularly NLP for low resourced languages; Machine Translation for low resourced languages; and I am also intersted in Machine learning and Deep learning researches.

Open for collaboration and Internship opportunities
